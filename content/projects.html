Projects

  • Stewart Platform: Designed the circuitry and code for a parallel plate 6-DOF motion platform, commonly known as a Stewart Platform. This project made use of the closed chain kinematic equations found in the Modern Robotics textbook.
  • Robotic Arm School Project: Designed and built a 6-DOF servo actuate arm capable of writing a sequence of 4 letters using the first 10 letters of the alphabet in any order.
