About

I am an engineer with a diverse background unified by a common thread, robotics. While my formal education was in mechanical engineering, my love for robotics has furthered my knowledge in electronics, computer science, networking, and control theory. On my free time, I enjoy continued learning through online courses, tinkering with new technology, occasionally writing music, and hiking as often as possible! I have demonstrated ability to adapt to my environment and grow from new challenges. This has lead me to be a successful engineer with Novus Labs as I am able to excel in a fast pace, dynamic workplace. I am currently thrilled to be working on a robotics project with Novus Labs and Cruise, but am always open to new opportunities!
